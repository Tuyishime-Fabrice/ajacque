Frequently Asked

Questions & Answers

Find answers to the most common questions about our services, ordering process, and delivery.

Ordering Process
How do I place an order for a coffin?
+
You can place an order by calling or WhatsApp-ing us at +250 788 871 345, visiting our showroom on KG 14 Avenue, or by using the contact form on this website. Our team will guide you through the selection process with patience and care.
Can I place an emergency order at night?
+
Yes. Our emergency line is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Simply call +250 788 871 345 at any hour and a team member will respond immediately to assist your family.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept cash (RWF), mobile money (MTN MoMo and Airtel Money), and bank transfers. We work with families to ensure payment arrangements are as stress-free as possible during a difficult time.
Customisation
Can I request a custom-sized coffin?
+
Absolutely. We accommodate custom dimensions, interior lining choices, wood finishes, and other personalisation options. Please contact us with your specific requirements and we will advise on timeline and pricing.
What materials do your coffins come in?
+
Our collection includes premium hardwoods such as mahogany, oak, and select local timber. Interior linings are available in various fabrics. Each option is carefully selected for quality and durability.
Delivery
Do you deliver outside of Kigali?
+
Yes. While our showroom is in Kigali, we provide delivery services across Rwanda. Delivery fees and timelines vary by distance. Please contact us to discuss your specific location and we will arrange accordingly.
How quickly can you deliver?
+
For standard orders within Kigali, delivery is typically arranged within the same day or next day. For emergency situations, we prioritise delivery as quickly as possible. Custom orders may require additional production time.
General
Are your prices fixed or negotiable?
+
Our prices reflect the quality of materials and craftsmanship invested in each coffin. We always aim to be transparent about pricing. For families facing financial hardship, we are open to a respectful conversation about what is possible.
Where are you located?
+
Our showroom and workshop are located at KG 14 Avenue, Kigali, Rwanda. You are welcome to visit during business hours (Monday to Saturday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM) or call ahead for an appointment.

Entity Relationship Overview

The platform uses a relational SQL database with 9 core tables. Products belong to categories and have multiple images. Contact messages and orders are stored independently. Admin accounts are managed separately with session-based authentication.

Authentication Architecture

Admin authentication uses session-based login with bcrypt password hashing. Sessions expire after 8 hours of inactivity. Login attempts are rate-limited to prevent brute force attacks. HttpOnly cookies store the session token.

MechanismDetail
Password Storagebcrypt with cost factor 12
SessionServer-side session with HttpOnly + Secure cookie
Session Expiry8 hours idle timeout
Rate Limiting5 failed attempts triggers 15-min lockout
CSRF ProtectionToken-based CSRF on all state-changing requests
